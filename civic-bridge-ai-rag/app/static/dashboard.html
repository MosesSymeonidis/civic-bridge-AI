Reading this as a policy maker

CM/Rec(2022)16 calls for graduated and differentiated responses. A severity mix dominated by tiers 1 and 2 points to education, awareness and counter-speech measures; recurring tier 3 signals justify strengthening reporting channels and human review capacity; tier 4 signals concern conduct that may fall under criminal law and belong with the competent authorities. The mechanism chart shows what dialogue repair should target in schools and public communication.

Civic Bridge AI is a human-review aid for education and civic response. Aggregates can be shared without enabling identification of any person, school, or small local group.
